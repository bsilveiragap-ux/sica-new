1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognise your device on future visits and improve your browsing experience.

On this website we primarily use the browser's localStorage — not HTTP cookies — to record your consent preferences.

2. Why do we use cookies?

We use cookies and similar technologies to ensure the website functions correctly, analyse how it is used, and support our marketing activities. You can manage your preferences at any time.

The processing of personal data for analytics and marketing purposes is carried out based on your explicit consent, pursuant to Article 6(1)(a) of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR — Regulation (EU) 2016/679).

Essential cookies are used on the basis of legitimate interest, as they are necessary for the operation of the website.

You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before such withdrawal.
