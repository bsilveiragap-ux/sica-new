General Medicine · Lisbon 01
+42%

qualified leads in 90 days

General practice clinic with an unstable schedule and total reliance on referrals. No active digital presence. Goal: replace word-of-mouth growth with a continuous, predictable flow of new patients.

What was done

  1. Google Ads restructure with segmentation by specialty and search intent — we eliminated waste and focused the budget on terms with the highest conversion rate.
  2. Dedicated landing page with copy focused on the patient's problem, social proof and a direct CTA for online booking.
  3. Lead tracking system by source and quality — weekly reports with cost per lead, cost per booking and attendance rate.

Dental Clinic · Porto 02
4.2×

ROAS achieved in 60 days of campaigns

Dental clinic investing in Meta Ads without measurable return. Great offer, wrong channel. We migrated the strategy to Google Ads with a funnel structure by treatment type and precise targeting.

What was done

  1. Full migration and restructure to Google Ads — separate campaigns per treatment (implants, orthodontics, aesthetic dentistry) with specific copy and bids for each service.
  2. Remarketing strategy for unconverted leads with custom creatives per treatment type and decision funnel stage.
  3. Bid optimisation based on real weekly ROAS — eliminating non-performing campaigns and scaling those that exceeded the target.
4.2× Average ROAS in 60 days
✓ More qualified patients
✓ Revenue growing month on month
Aesthetic Clinic · Braga 03
60d

to a fully booked schedule

Newly opened aesthetic clinic with no digital presence and no campaign history. Controlled budget. Goal: generate appointment volume quickly and build organic growth in parallel.

What was done

  1. Combination of Google Ads for immediate volume and local SEO for lasting organic visibility — both channels feeding each other from the first month.
  2. Content production oriented to search intent: treatment comparisons, expectations, prices and recovery — the real questions patients search before booking.
  3. Rigorous weekly optimisation cycle — creatives, audiences and offers tested in 7 days, eliminating what doesn't convert and scaling what works.
