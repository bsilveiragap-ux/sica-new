Dispute Resolution

Pursuant to Portuguese Law no. 144/2015 of 8 September

Amicable resolution

In the event of a dispute, Eclipse D'Letras Unipessoal, LDA (Sica Creative) is committed to seeking an amicable resolution before pursuing any other avenue. Please contact us directly:

Email: info@sicacreative.com

Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR)

Under Portuguese Law no. 144/2015 of 8 September, in the event of a consumer dispute, you may refer the matter to an Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) entity. The competent entities for consumer dispute mediation in Portugal are:

Centro de Arbitragem de Conflitos de Consumo de Lisboa (CACCL)

Rua dos Douradores, 116 – 2.º, 1100-207 Lisboa, Portugal

Tel.: +351 218 807 030

www.centroarbitragemlisboa.pt
CNIACC – National Centre for Consumer Dispute Information and Arbitration

Universidade Nova de Lisboa, Campus de Campolide, 1099-032 Lisboa, Portugal

Tel.: +351 213 847 484

www.cniacc.pt
CICAP – Consumer Information and Arbitration Centre of Porto

Rua Damião de Góis, 31 – Loja 6, 4050-225 Porto, Portugal

Tel.: +351 225 508 349

www.cicap.pt
CACCOA – Consumer Dispute Arbitration Centre of the Algarve

Ninho de Empresas, Edifício NERA, Estrada da Penha, 8005-131 Faro, Portugal

Tel.: +351 289 813 533

www.consumidoronline.pt

European online dispute resolution platform

For cross-border disputes within the European Union, you may also use the European Commission's online dispute resolution platform:

ec.europa.eu/consumers/odr
