2. Data we collect

We collect the following personal data when you interact with our website:

  • Contact data: name, email and phone number provided through forms
  • Browsing data: IP address, browser type, pages visited and visit duration (via Google Analytics)
  • Marketing data: interactions with advertising campaigns (via Google Ads and Meta Pixel)
  • Communication data: email address for sending communications (via Omnisend)

We do not collect sensitive data (special categories) as defined in Article 9 of the GDPR.

3. Purposes and legal basis

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Responding to contact requests — legal basis: pre-contractual measures (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR)
  • Sending marketing communications — legal basis: consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR)
  • Traffic analysis and website improvement — legal basis: consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR)
  • Measuring advertising campaigns — legal basis: consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR)
  • Compliance with legal obligations — legal basis: legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR)

4. Retention periods

Your data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected:

  • Prospective client contact data: up to 2 years after last contact
  • Active client data: duration of contract + 10 years (tax obligation)
  • Marketing data (email): until unsubscription
  • Browsing data (Analytics): 14 months (Google Analytics 4 default)

5. Sharing data with third parties

Your data may be shared with the following processors, solely for the purposes indicated:

  • Google LLC — traffic analysis (Google Analytics) and advertising (Google Ads)
  • Meta Platforms, Inc. — advertising and remarketing (Meta Pixel)
  • Omnisend — email marketing automation
  • Calendly — meeting scheduling

All processors provide sufficient guarantees of GDPR compliance. We do not sell or transfer your data to third parties for their own commercial purposes.

Some of the processors above are based outside the European Economic Area (EEA). Data transfers are carried out under appropriate safeguards, namely the Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

6. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access Know what data we hold about you
Rectification Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
Erasure Request deletion of your data
Portability Receive your data in a readable format
Objection Object to processing for marketing
Restriction Restrict processing in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@sicacreative.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority in Portugal:

CNPD — Comissão Nacional de Proteção de Dados
www.cnpd.pt

7. Data security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, destruction or accidental disclosure. Access to data is restricted to staff with a need to know.

8. Cookies and similar technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies. For detailed information about the cookies we use and how to manage them, please consult our Cookie Policy.
